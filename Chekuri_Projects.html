Interstellar Battlestar

September 19 - October 6, Intro to Computer Science and Software Engineering

In this project, my partner and I created a game using App Inventor. We used the game "Space Invaders" to build the basis of our game and then used App Inventor to remix it into our own game. We decided to make our app a retro-Galaxy themed shooter game in which the user can control their battle ship side to side and shoot upwards towards alien saucers that are coming down towards the ship. The game lasted exactly one minute, progressively getting more difficult, and the user had to hit enemy saucers to gain points. Hitting objects other than enemy saucers resulted in negative points. There was a health bar on the bottom of the screen representing the Galactic Empire's health which decreased every time an enemy saucer or a harmful object was not shot by the user on time. The game ended if the user survived one minute or the health bar reduced completely to 0. We were also able to add a quick tutorial in the beginning of the game to show users how to play.

PixelPaws

March 6 - March 21, Intro to Computer Science and Software Engineering

In this project, my partner and I manipulted images to fit the needs of our client, Norsled, a rescue organization that focuses on fostering and finding homes for unwanted Northern dogs, such as Huskies, Alaskan Malamutes, Samoyeds, etc.We helped our client through adding their organization's logo onto the corner of images to identify the shelter dogs as theirs, and also created a border that matched the colors of the organization's logo around the images. Norsled can change the position of the watermark through modifying the parameters depending on the image, so that the watermark does not cover an important part of the image. Norsled can also change the frame size and color around the image to better suit the image and the page the image is displayed on. Furthermore, Norsled can change the dimensions of the images and resize them all to the same dimensions in whatever way makes their website more aesthetically pleasing to their audience.
